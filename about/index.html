What Is The Event About?

This event promises a fun filled experience for your children. The children will be dressing like their favourite cartoon characters or in career clothing (for example nurse, doctor, or even a princess). There will be lots of games, prizes, and rides such as bouncy castles and slides. There will be face painting, horseback riding, and a petting zoo. Dont worry about them getting hungry. All of their favourite fruits, food, snacks, and drinks will be provided. Hopefully you love giveaways because 2 tablets will be given away each month. We also have your children's security in mind as we will have police presence at the event. Also, if by any chance you would like to keep an eye on what is happening and ensure the safety of your children, a web cammera will be setup for parents to monitor their children online. You can view some answers to common questions, some food that will be available at the event, and how you can register your child in the sections below.

Answers to Common Questions and Concerns

Below are some answers to some common questions or concerns that you may have. If you still have any concerns after reading this list, please contact us via our contact information on the Contact page.

  1. There will be a camera for parents to monitor their children online if they feel the need to.
  2. All children will be given name tags and a tick will be placed on their name tag if they are allergic to anything.
  3. A contact phone number will also be placed on the child's name tag in the case of emergency.
  4. All children will be placed in seperate tents with several caretakers for the day.
  5. There will be 4 first aid nurses everyday at the event.
  6. The children will not be allowed to leave the tent without a caretaker.
  7. Food, drinks, and snacks will be brought by the caretaker.
  8. All rides and activites will be carried out in groups, which will be named and numbered and your children will be assigned to everyday.
  9. All children will be in their mask and will sanitize their hands entering the event and before eating.
  10. Note that police will be on site everyday..

How Can I Register My Children For The Event?

You must ensure to follow the steps below in order for your children to be registered for the event:

  1. First you need to navigate to the Forms section below and fill out the registration form for your child. There are two different forms, one for 3 to 4 year olds and one for 5 to 11 year olds. If you are registering more than one child you must fill out a seperate form for each of them. Please ensure that all the information that you put on these forms are up to date.
