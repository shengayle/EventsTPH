Introducing the Summer Freedom Festival

Attention all parents and guardians! You can't afford for your children to miss out on this exciting event! This Summer Thifa's Party Harty Event will be hosting a Summer Freedom Festival for children between the ages of 3 and 11. The festival will take place in the July to August vocation and it will be located in Kitchiner Park, Port of Spain. It promises an awesome experience for the children including dress up, lots of games and prizes, etc. It is a chance for the children to enjoy themselves while you get a moment to relax as well. Sounds interesting so far?
